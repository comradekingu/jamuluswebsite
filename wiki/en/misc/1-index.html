Jamulus?

Play together remotely. Collaborative high quality, low-latency sound via broadband — with friends, your band, or someone found online.

Worldwide

Huge choirs, rock bands, folk and classical musicians, remote music lessons, in schools and universities, private or public. Even the spoken word — as if you were there in person.

Help?

Check out the documentation. Especially the troubleshooting section. You can also ask on the forums.

Get involved

Use, see, change, and share — with all. write code or translate it on Weblate.
The contribution guidelines has more info.

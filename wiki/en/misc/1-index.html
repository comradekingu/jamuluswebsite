Jamulus?

Set it up now and play together remotely. Collaborative high quality, low-latency sound via broadband. With friends, your band, or someone found online.

Jamulus worldwide

Huge choirs, rock bands, folk and classical musicians, remote music lessons, in schools and universities, private or public. Even the spoken word — as if you were there in person.

Help needed?

Check out the documentation. Especially the troubleshooting! You can also ask on the forums.

Get involved

Use, see, change, and share — with all. write code or translate it on Weblate.
The contribution guidelines has more info.

Info on the 5th dimension of collaborative sound to be found in this paper by Volker Fischer (PDF).
